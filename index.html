About us

BARBERSHOP co is a barbershop located in Ukraine. We specialize in wooden constructions made from the ground up. We only use the best materials for our work. All projects are developed using old school carpentry methods.

Brief info

We provide clean and professional cut depending on an event you are going to. Our professionals are highly specialized in barber shop sphere so we will make everything as cool and as fast as possible.
